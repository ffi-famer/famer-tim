PROJECT X Operational Design Domain 
===

<treqs-element id="727a144c3ffd11f193ca467a017f3d7e" type="operational-design-domain">

Collision Avoidance by Steering (CAS) ODD
---

**Geographic Scope:**

- European public roads (highways, rural, and urban)

**Road types:**

- Marked lanes with visible line symmetry
- Structured traffic environments
- Excludes:
  - Off-road driving
  - Unpaved or construction zones with no lane structure

**Weather Conditions:**

- Dry road conditions
- Light to moderate rain
- Light snowfall
- Excludes:
  - Heavy fog or snow (visibility < 50m)
  - Severe weather obscuring lane or object detection

**Lighting Types:**

- Daylight
- Dusk/Dawn
- Night driving with standard vehicle lighting

**Speed range:**

- Urban: 30-90 km/h
- Highway: 100-130 km/h
- Excludes:
  - Speeds beyond 130 km/h

**Operational constraints:**

- Requires visible lane markings for path planning
- Requires functional object detection and tracking
