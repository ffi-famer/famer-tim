**6.4.2** Software safety requirements derived from the technical safety requirements, technical safety concept, and system architectural design shall consider:

a) specification and management of safety requirements;
b) specified system and hardware configurations;
c) the hardware-software interface specification;
d) relevant hardware design requirements;
e) timing constraints;
f) external interfaces; and
g) operating modes and transitions affecting the software.

**6.4.7** The software safety requirements and the refined hardware-software interface requirements shall be verified to provide evidence of:

a) suitability for software development;
b) compliance and consistency with the technical safety requirements;
c) compliance with the system design; and
d) consistency with the hardware-software interface.
