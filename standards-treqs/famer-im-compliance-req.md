<treqs-element id="bf37f1b6dc6b4ffb8b41232a8f6e39c4" type="compl.requirement">

FAMER TIM should be aligned with FAMER IM
===

</treqs-element>

<treqs-element id="a908958df06f4c9795e421a1f68755dc" type="compl.requirement">

FAMER TIM should be as easy to use as possible
---

- Simpler than FAMER IM (less artifacts, less links)
- Consistent naming and link style

</treqs-element>

<treqs-element id="90dd952b69d8428588e593594b3ef107" type="compl.requirement">

FAMER TIM shall not contradict or over-simplify relevant standards
---

Specifically, the FAMER TIM shall be consistent with ISO 8800 and ISO 21448.

</treqs-element>
<treqs-element id="69c0ff8e30134ffdbcb8bb0b645907cb" type="compl.requirement">

FAMER TIM shall be validated with experts from FAMER consortium
---

Feedback from iterative validation workshops may lead to FAMER design decisions that are then motivated in this way.
The concrete feedback will be either logged in the design decision or in famer-tim-rationale.md.
</treqs-element>
