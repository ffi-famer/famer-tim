ISO-8800-R1: AI safety reqt derived from system level safety reqt
--

AI safety requirements shall be derived from system-level safety requirements, and refined to address:

- Functional insufficiencies (output errors, misclassifications).
- Systematic faults.
- Random hardware faults.

ISO-8800-R2: Measurable KPIs for AI safety requirements
--

Each AI safety requirement shall include measurable Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), as quantitative evidence requires the definition of a set of metrics and target thresholds.

ISO-8800-R3: Traceability between safety and data requirements
--

Bidirectional traceability shall be maintained between:

- System-level safety requirements and the corresponding AI safety requirements.
- AI safety requirements and data set requirements.

--

The AI input space shall be explicitly defined during requirements phase, and maintained and updated throghout development.

ISO-8800-R5: Development measures in AI model training
--

The AI model training shall incorporate the following appropriate development measures:
    - Fault-aware training, hyperparameter tuning, robust learning, transfer learning, confidence calibration and uncertainty qualification, and augmentation of data.
