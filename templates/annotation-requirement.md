### Summary

> In this section, one would expect a specification of what should be labeled and annotation quality criteria.
> Annotation requirements augment data-set requirements with concrete labeling specifications.

### Technical Details

> In this section, all annotation requirements shall be documented with the following aspects:

#### 1. Label Types and Classes

> Classes / entities that require annotation (e.g 'pedestrians', 'traffic signs')

#### 2. Annotation Attributes

> Required attributes for each labeled entity (e.g 'bounding box', 'visibility score')

#### 3. Quality Criteria

> Accuracy thresholds and consistency requirements.

#### 4. Input space Constraints

> ODD-related constraints that may affect annotation (e.g 'must cover adverse weather conditions').
> See ISO-8800, Clause 9.5.3.

### Known Limitations

> Known limitations should be documented with or alongside annotation requirements (analytical limitations) and annotation guidelines (practical limitations).
> See ISO-21448, Clause 5.4 and 5.5.
